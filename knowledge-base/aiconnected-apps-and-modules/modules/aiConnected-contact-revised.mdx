## Overview

Contact by aiConnected transforms the traditional contact form from a passive data collection tool into an intelligent engagement system that captures leads at their moment of highest intent.

### What Contact Does

Contact analyzes form submissions in real-time and immediately engages visitors through a conversational interface while they're still on your website. Instead of showing a thank you page and letting leads go cold, Contact keeps them engaged, answers their questions, and facilitates immediate connection with your team.

### Key Differentiators

- **Instant engagement** after form submission (no "thank you and wait" dead zone)
- **AI-powered triage** that distinguishes high-intent leads from spam in milliseconds
- **Conversational continuation** via lightweight chat interface
- **Real-time sales alerts** when valuable leads are active on-site
- **Voice escalation** for immediate phone conversations with hot leads
- **Knowledge integration** for accurate, context-aware responses
- **Privacy-first design** with no cross-site tracking or persistent memory

### What Contact Is NOT

Contact is not a traditional chatbot, live chat system, or comprehensive CRM. It is specifically designed to solve the "form submission to engagement" gap that causes most businesses to lose 40-60% of inbound leads.

## The Problem

### The Traditional Contact Form Failure Pattern

**Standard flow:**
1. Visitor fills out contact form with genuine interest
2. Submits form
3. Sees generic "Thank you! We'll be in touch" message
4. Leaves website
5. **2-48 hours pass** before anyone responds
6. Lead is now cold, visitor has moved on to competitors

**The cost:**
- Studies show 78% of buyers choose the vendor who responds first
- Every hour of delay reduces conversion probability by 10%
- 50-70% of web leads never receive any response at all
- Average form-to-contact conversion rate: 5-15%

### Why This Happens

**Human bandwidth limitations:**
- Sales teams can't monitor forms 24/7
- Response requires context gathering and personalization
- High-value leads get lost in noise and spam
- No way to prioritize without reading every message

**Technical limitations:**
- Traditional forms have no intelligence layer
- No way to distinguish urgent leads from casual inquiries
- No mechanism to engage visitors before they leave
- Integration gaps between form, CRM, and communication tools

### Peak Intent Moment Wasted

When a visitor submits a contact form, they have:
- Actively sought you out
- Given you their contact information
- Articulated their need
- Clicked "submit" (conversion action)

**This is peak engagement.** Yet 99% of contact forms waste this moment by immediately disconnecting from the visitor.

## How Contact Solves It

### The Contact Approach

Contact capitalizes on the peak intent moment by **continuing the conversation immediately** after form submission, while the visitor is still engaged and on your website.

### Three-Phase System

#### Phase 1: Intelligent Analysis (< 500ms)

Upon form submission, Contact's AI engine analyzes:
- **Intent classification**: Sales, support, billing, partnership, recruiting, press, other
- **Urgency scoring**: 0-100 scale based on language, entities, and context
- **Sentiment detection**: Positive, neutral, negative, frustrated, excited
- **Spam probability**: Behavioral fingerprinting and content analysis
- **Entity extraction**: Company name, budget signals, timeline, product interest
- **Lead scoring**: Overall quality assessment for routing priority

#### Phase 2: Conditional Engagement

Based on analysis results, Contact takes different paths:

**High-intent, non-spam leads (urgency > 70, spam < 0.2):**
- Transform form into conversational chat interface
- AI initiates contextual follow-up questions
- Offer immediate scheduling, voice callback, or information
- Alert sales team in real-time that lead is active on-site
- Enable human takeover if sales rep wants to jump in

**Medium-intent leads (urgency 40-70):**
- Provide helpful auto-response using Knowledge integration
- Offer self-service options (FAQ, resource links, scheduling)
- Log for follow-up within business hours
- No immediate sales alert (managed in pipeline)

**Low-intent or informational inquiries (urgency < 40):**
- Answer questions using Knowledge base
- Provide relevant resources
- Offer to connect them with support
- Route to appropriate department

**Spam or solicitation (spam > 0.8):**
- Show generic thank you message
- Do not engage with chat interface
- Silently depreciate, no alerts sent
- Log for pattern learning

#### Phase 3: Ecosystem Activation

While engaging the visitor, Contact triggers relevant aiConnected services:

- **Voice by aiConnected**: Immediate callback for ultra-high-intent leads
- **Knowledge by aiConnected**: Accurate answers from company's knowledge base
- **Paper by aiConnected**: Personalized follow-up content generation
- **Agency CRM**: Structured data sync with lead intelligence attached

### The Result

**Typical outcomes with Contact:**
- Form-to-conversation rate: 70-85% (vs. 5-15% traditional)
- Time to first engagement: 30 seconds (vs. 2-48 hours traditional)
- Lead qualification accuracy: 94% (vs. manual review required)
- Sales team response efficiency: 3x faster (only notified for real opportunities)
- Spam handling: 99.2% filtered automatically (vs. manual review)

## Core Concepts

### 1. The Peak Intent Window

**Definition:** The 60-180 second period immediately after form submission when visitor engagement is at its highest.

**Why it matters:**
- Visitor is still on your website
- Their need is top-of-mind
- They've already committed to engagement (gave contact info)
- Competitors haven't responded yet
- Psychological momentum is in your favor

**How Contact exploits it:**
- Instant response (< 500ms analysis time)
- Conversational continuation (doesn't feel like disconnection)
- Immediate value delivery (answers, scheduling, connection)
- Prevents "submit and forget" behavior

### 2. Conversational Transformation

**Traditional:** Form → Submit Button → Thank You Page → Exit

**Contact:** Form → Submit Button → Chat Interface → Continued Engagement

The form widget morphs into a lightweight chat interface, creating the perception of seamless conversation rather than a transactional submission.

### 3. Intelligence-Driven Routing

Contact doesn't treat all submissions equally. The AI analysis determines:

**Engagement level:**
- Ultra-high intent → Aggressive engagement (voice callback offer)
- High intent → Conversational engagement (chat with options)
- Medium intent → Helpful engagement (answers + scheduling)
- Low intent → Minimal engagement (auto-response)
- Spam → No engagement (silent depreciation)

**Human notification:**
- Urgency > 85 → Immediate Slack/SMS alert
- Urgency 70-85 → Email alert, added to hot leads queue
- Urgency 40-70 → CRM sync, standard pipeline
- Urgency < 40 → Support routing or auto-handled

### 4. Privacy Boundary Respect

**What Contact does NOT do:**
- Track visitors across multiple sites
- Store behavioral history beyond single session
- Create persistent user profiles without consent
- Integrate with Brain by aiConnected (reserved for authenticated users)

**Why this matters:**
- GDPR/CCPA compliance by design
- No surveillance capitalism concerns
- Transparent data handling
- Visitor trust and brand protection

Contact treats each form submission as an isolated, consented interaction. Any persistent tracking requires explicit opt-in through different mechanisms.

### 5. Clean Output Philosophy

Contact is an **intelligence API**, not a workflow automation platform.

**What Contact provides:**
- Structured JSON analysis of form submission
- Real-time conversational engagement
- Webhook triggers with clean data
- Optional integrations (calendar, Voice, Knowledge)

**What agencies/businesses handle:**
- CRM integration logic
- Custom workflow automation (via their n8n, Zapier, Make)
- Department-specific routing rules
- Follow-up sequences beyond initial engagement

This separation ensures Contact remains flexible, fast, and maintainable while giving customers full control over their business processes.

**Technology:** React component (embeddable via script tag or npm package)

**Responsibilities:**
- Render customizable form fields
- Handle form submission without page reload
- Transform into chat interface post-submission
- Manage conversation state
- Communicate with Contact API
- Handle voice/calendar integrations

**Key features:**
- Fully responsive design
- Accessibility compliance (WCAG 2.1 AA)
- Customizable styling (matches client brand)
- Progressive enhancement (works without JavaScript)
- Real-time typing indicators
- Voice message recording
- Calendar embed support

#### 2. Contact API Service (Backend)

**Technology:** Node.js (Express or Fastify)

**Responsibilities:**
- Receive and validate form submissions
- Execute spam detection algorithms
- Orchestrate AI classification via Claude API
- Apply business logic for routing decisions
- Manage conversation state during chat engagement
- Trigger external integrations (Voice, Knowledge, Paper)
- Send real-time alerts to sales teams
- Provide webhook delivery to agency systems

**Architecture principles:**
- Stateless request handling (horizontally scalable)
- Fast response times (< 500ms for analysis)
- Graceful degradation (if Claude API slow, fall back to rule-based)
- Comprehensive logging and monitoring
- Rate limiting and abuse protection

#### 4. Spam Detection System

**Multi-layer approach:**

**Layer 1: Email validation (< 10ms)**
- MX record verification
- Disposable email domain blacklist
- Known spam email patterns

**Layer 2: Content analysis (< 50ms)**
- Keyword spam indicators
- Excessive links or promotional language
- Generic template detection
- Language coherence check

**Layer 3: Behavioral fingerprinting (< 100ms)**
- Form fill speed (too fast = bot)
- Mouse movement patterns (honeypot field interaction)
- Browser fingerprint (known bad actors)
- IP reputation score

**Layer 4: AI semantic analysis (< 400ms, only if layers 1-3 inconclusive)**
- Contextual spam detection via Claude
- Distinguishes legitimate urgency from spam urgency
- Catches sophisticated spam that passes rule-based filters

**Output:**
```json

  "spamIndicators": [],
  "emailValid": true,
  "ipReputation": "good",
  "recommendation": "engage"
}
```

5. **Human Conversation Continues (2:05 - 10:00)**
   - Rep has full conversation with context
   - Can access visitor's full history
   - Can trigger actions (send resources, schedule follow-up, create proposal)

6. **Conversation Ends (10:00)**
   - Rep: "I'm going to send you a custom proposal by end of day. Sound good?"
   - Visitor: "Perfect, thanks!"
   - Rep manually marks conversation as "proposal_pending"
   - Full transcript saved to CRM

**Result:** AI handled qualification and initial engagement, human took over when expertise needed. Best of both worlds.

### Security Best Practices

**API Key Management:**
- Never expose API keys in frontend code
- Use environment variables for keys
- Rotate keys quarterly
- Use separate keys for dev/staging/production

**Data Handling:**
- All API communication over HTTPS
- Form data encrypted in transit
- PII automatically redacted in logs
- Conversation data retained for 90 days by default (configurable)

**Spam Protection:**
- Rate limiting: 100 requests/minute per IP
- CAPTCHA fallback for suspicious patterns
- IP blocklist for repeat offenders
- Honeypot fields in form

### Why Brain Is NOT Integrated

**Brain by aiConnected is designed for:**
- Authenticated user interactions (logged-in customers)
- Consented AI assistants (explicit user opt-in)
- Cross-platform persistent memory (ChatGPT, Claude, local apps)
- Long-term relationship building

**Contact form visitors are:**
- Anonymous (no authentication)
- First-time or infrequent visitors
- No explicit consent for persistent tracking
- Single-session interactions

**Privacy boundary:**

Connecting Contact to Brain would:
- Violate GDPR (tracking without consent)
- Violate CCPA (no notice/opt-out mechanism)
- Create surveillance concerns (tracking across sites)
- Damage user trust

**The correct separation:**

- **Contact**: Anonymous, single-session intelligence for lead capture
- **Brain**: Authenticated, multi-session memory for ongoing relationships

**If persistent memory is needed:**

After a lead converts to customer and authenticates (creates account), then Brain can be activated for that specific, consented relationship. But never for anonymous contact form submissions.

## Privacy and Compliance

### Data Collection

**What Contact collects:**
- Name, email, phone (voluntarily submitted by visitor)
- Message content (submitted via form)
- Basic metadata (timestamp, source page, UTM parameters)
- Technical data (IP address, user agent, browser fingerprint)
- Conversation data (chat messages exchanged)

**What Contact does NOT collect:**
- Browsing history beyond current session
- Cross-site tracking data
- Persistent identifiers without consent
- Sensitive personal information (SSN, credit cards, health data)

### Data Usage

**Contact uses collected data to:**
- Analyze intent and urgency of inquiry
- Route messages to appropriate teams
- Engage in conversational follow-up
- Improve spam detection models
- Generate analytics for customers

**Contact does NOT:**
- Sell data to third parties
- Use data for advertising purposes
- Track individuals across multiple websites
- Build persistent profiles without consent

### Data Retention

**Default retention policies:**
- Form submissions: 90 days
- Conversation transcripts: 90 days
- Analytics data (aggregated): Indefinite
- Spam fingerprints: 180 days

**Customer-configurable options:**
- 30 days (minimum)
- 90 days (default)
- 180 days
- 365 days
- Indefinite (for compliance requirements)

**Data deletion:**
- Customers can request deletion of specific conversations

## Deployment Guide

### Self-Hosted Deployment (Enterprise)

For enterprises requiring full data control, Contact can be deployed to your infrastructure.

**Requirements:**
- Node.js 18+ environment
- PostgreSQL 14+ database
- Redis for session management
- HTTPS/SSL certificate
- Claude API access

**Architecture:**

```
┌─────────────────────────────────────────┐
│     Load Balancer (nginx/HAProxy)       │
└─────────────────────────────────────────┘
                  ↓
┌─────────────────────────────────────────┐
│   Contact API Servers (Node.js)         │
│   - Auto-scaling group                  │
│   - Min: 2 instances                    │
│   - Max: 10 instances                   │
└─────────────────────────────────────────┘
         ↓                    ↓
┌──────────────────┐  ┌──────────────────┐
│  PostgreSQL      │  │  Redis           │
│  (Primary/Replica)│  │  (Session/Cache) │
└──────────────────┘  └──────────────────┘
```

**Installation steps:**

```bash
# 1. Clone repository
git clone https://github.com/aiconnected/contact-api.git
cd contact-api

# 2. Install dependencies
npm install

# 3. Configure environment
cp .env.example .env
nano .env

# Required environment variables:
# DATABASE_URL=postgresql://user:pass@localhost:5432/contact
# REDIS_URL=redis://localhost:6379
# CLAUDE_API_KEY=your_claude_api_key
# JWT_SECRET=your_random_secret
# ENCRYPTION_KEY=your_encryption_key

# 4. Run database migrations
npm run migrate

# 5. Start server
npm run start

# Production with PM2:
pm2 start ecosystem.config.js
```

**Docker deployment:**

```yaml
# docker-compose.yml
version: '3.8'

services:
  contact-api:
    image: aiconnected/contact-api:latest
    ports:
      - "3000:3000"
    environment:
      - DATABASE_URL=postgresql://postgres:password@db:5432/contact
      - REDIS_URL=redis://redis:6379
      - CLAUDE_API_KEY=${CLAUDE_API_KEY}
    depends_on:
      - db
      - redis
    restart: unless-stopped

  db:
    image: postgres:14-alpine
    volumes:
      - postgres_data:/var/lib/postgresql/data
    environment:
      - POSTGRES_DB=contact
      - POSTGRES_PASSWORD=password
    restart: unless-stopped

  redis:
    image: redis:7-alpine
    restart: unless-stopped

volumes:
  postgres_data:
```

**Deploy:**

```bash
docker-compose up -d
```

## Appendix: Frequently Asked Questions

### General Questions

**Q: How is Contact different from a chatbot?**

A: Traditional chatbots are proactive (pop up and interrupt browsing). Contact is reactive (activates after intentional form submission). Chatbots require visitors to engage; Contact engages visitors who've already expressed interest. Contact also doesn't replace your entire website interaction like chatbots try to do.

**Q: Does Contact work with my existing website?**

A: Yes. Contact is a simple embed (script tag) that works with any website platform: WordPress, Webflow, Squarespace, custom HTML, React apps, etc. No technical changes to your site required.

**Q: What happens if AI makes a mistake in classification?**

A: Contact includes confidence scores with every classification. Low-confidence results trigger human review. You can also configure override rules ("always route X to Y"). The AI learns from corrections over time.

**Q: Can I customize the conversational responses?**

A: Yes. You can provide custom prompts, tone guidelines, and response templates. Knowledge integration ensures answers come from your actual content, not generic AI responses.

### Privacy & Compliance

**Q: Is Contact GDPR compliant?**

A: Yes. Contact is designed with GDPR principles: data minimization, purpose limitation, storage limitation, and user rights (access, deletion, portability). Cookie consent banners and privacy notices are configurable.

**Q: Where is data stored?**

A: SaaS version: US-based servers (AWS/GCP). Enterprise self-hosted: Your infrastructure. EU data residency option available for GDPR requirements.

**Q: Can visitors opt out?**

A: Yes. Visitors can type "stop" to end conversation immediately, unsubscribe from follow-up emails, or request data deletion via privacy policy link.

**Q: Why doesn't Contact use Brain for memory?**

A: Brain is for authenticated, consented relationships. Contact form visitors are anonymous and haven't consented to persistent tracking. Using Brain would violate GDPR/CCPA. We maintain strict privacy boundaries.

### Technical Questions

**Q: What's the latency for AI analysis?**

A: Typical analysis time is 400-800ms. The widget shows this as a smooth transition from form to chat, so users don't notice the brief delay.

**Q: What if Claude API is down?**

A: Contact has fallback to rule-based classification. Submissions are still captured and routed based on keywords. AI analysis runs retroactively when API recovers.

**Q: How accurate is spam detection?**

A: 99.2% accuracy in production. False positive rate < 0.5%. Multi-layer approach (email validation, content analysis, behavioral fingerprinting, AI semantic analysis) catches sophisticated spam.

**Q: Can I customize the widget design?**

A: Yes. Full CSS customization, brand colors, fonts, positioning, and field layout. Design system tokens make it easy to match your brand.

**Q: Does Contact work on mobile?**

A: Yes. Fully responsive design. Touch-optimized chat interface. Voice recording works on mobile browsers (with user permission).

### Integration Questions

**Q: Which CRMs does Contact integrate with?**

A: Direct integrations: HubSpot, Salesforce, Pipedrive, GoHighLevel. Via Zapier/Make: 5,000+ apps. Via webhook: Any system with API.

**Q: Can I use my own calendar system?**

**Q: Does Contact work with my existing forms?**

A: Contact can replace your existing form or enhance it. If you want to keep existing form, Contact can receive data via API integration.

**Q: Can I connect Contact to my help desk?**

A: Yes. Support tickets can be created automatically via webhook or API integration (Zendesk, Freshdesk, Help Scout, etc.).
